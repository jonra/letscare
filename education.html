Education

Asylum seekers has no right to, but can be granted up to 175 hours of training in the Norwegian language. This is for people over 16 without right and obligation to do training in Norwegian.

The municipality is responsible for organising the training. Those municipalities who receive grants from the government, are obliged to offer language training to asylum seekers. The language training will be based on the national curriculum for adult language training. Completing the language course, will make you eligable for a language exam.

Asylum seekers who are granted recidency and work permit have the right and obligation to full-time training in basic qualifications and Norwegian language tuition through an introductory programme.
